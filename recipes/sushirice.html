Sushi rice

Description

This recipe makes the perfect fluffy moist sushi rice!

  • 5 dl sushi rice
  • 1.25 dl rice vine vinegar
  • 0.75 dl sugar
  • 0.5 tbs salt
  1. Rinse the rice 5-7 times.
  2. Let rice soak in water for 30 mins.
  3. Pour the water away. Add new water: 1.5 times the volume of rice, for example 6 dl rice -> 9 dl water.
  4. Heat up on stove until boiling.
  5. Immediately when boiling starts, reduce to small temp and let cook for about 10 mins.
  6. While rice is cooking, add vinegar, sugar and salt into a kettle and warm up constantly stirring, until sugar and salt have dissolved. Remove from heat.
  7. After 10 mins, remove the rice from heat and let stand under lid for another 10 mins.
  8. Move to big oven form or similar, add the sauce and mix gently. Let cool (can help cooling with a fan).
