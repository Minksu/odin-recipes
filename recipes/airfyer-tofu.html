Airfryer tofu

Description

Easy and delicious sticky tofu in the airfryer :-)

  • 1 package firm tofu
  • 3 tbs sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tbs japanese soy sauce
  • 1.5 tbs roasted sesame oil
  • 1 ts garlic powder
  • sesame seeds
  • spring onion to garnish
  1. Dry the tofu block with kitchen paper.
  2. Cut tofu into small cubes.
  3. Cut a piece of baking paper to cover the bottom of the airfryer.
  4. Add tofu cubes into the airfryer, cook in 180 degrees for 12 mins. Shake couple of times during cooking.
  5. While tofu is cooking, mix soy, sesame oil, sweet chili sauce and garlic powder together.
  6. After cooking, toss the tofu in the sauce and add back to the airfryer. Cook additional 2 mins.
  7. Top with sesame seeds and spring onion. Serve with sticky white rice.
